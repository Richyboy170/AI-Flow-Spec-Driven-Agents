# Tidlor / เงินติดล้อ — Structured Research Pack

Build-grounding research for the low-poly 3D explainer (`sandbox/tidlor-3d`).
All figures below were gathered **inline this session** by the brainstorm-research lead
against official Tidlor investor-relations (IR) pages. Visual/brand evidence was gathered
separately by `cs-visual-researcher` and lives in
`sandbox/tidlor-3d/assets/visual-manifest.json` (12 verified, provenance-stamped assets).

**Accuracy contract for downstream build:**
- No invented rates, loan amounts, fees, or exact branch counts.
- Every figure carries a source URL + as-of date, or is marked **illustrative** / **uncertain**.
- Company self-claims (e.g. "over 1,800 branches", "#1", "leading") are tagged
  **[company-claimed]** and kept separate from independently structural facts.
- **ประกันติดล้อ (Prakan TIDLOR) = HISTORICAL** (2022 brand, rebranded 2024).
  **Shield Insurance / Areegator / heygoody = CURRENT** sub-brands.

Research date: 2026-06-24.

From [IR company history & milestones](https://www.tidlorinvestor.com/en/corporate-information/companys-history-and-milestone) (as-of 2026-06-24):

| Year | Milestone | Status |

**The single most important distinction for the build:**
> **ประกันติดล้อ (Prakan TIDLOR)** is the **HISTORICAL** name of the in-house insurance
> brand (2022). It was **rebranded to Shield Insurance in 2024**. A station may show the
> gold ประกันติดล้อ mark *as heritage*, but must not present it as a current sub-brand.
> The current insurance sub-brands are **Shield, Areegator, heygoody**.

The gold ประกันติดล้อ mark is still visible on physical branch signage in the captured
ecosystem photo (`product-ecosystem-vehicles-883663c419.jpg`), which is why it persists
visually even though the brand has been renamed — present it as legacy/branch-signage.

## 7. Uncertainties / gaps (flagged)

1. **Exact branch count** — only "over 1,800" (company-claimed, rounded) is available on
   the pages fetched. No precise audited branch number was found on the IR pages fetched
   this session. **Resolution: keep illustrative; never assert an exact branch count.**
2. **Registered capital, head-office address, employee headcount** — not present on the IR
   general-information page fetched. Left out of the build rather than guessed.
3. **Establishment exact date** — IR history gives the year (2006) only; the secondary
   web result's "22 Sept 2006 / 24 Oct 2006 / CFG Services" specifics are
   **secondary-sourced** and NOT used as fact. Only the IR-confirmed year 2006 is used.
4. **Original NTL IPO size (THB 38–43bn)** — secondary-source only; **excluded** from
   build copy (no IR confirmation fetched).
5. **Shield Insurance standalone visual mark** — no standalone Shield site/logo was
   reachable; the Shield wordmark was confirmed only inside the earnings key-visual
   (`brand-world-earnings-4subbrands-948b2823a2.jpg`). No dedicated Shield asset exists.
6. **Krungsri / Bank of Ayudhya relationship** — secondary results mention it; not
   confirmed on fetched IR pages; **excluded**.
